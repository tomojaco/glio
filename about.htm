Gotta Let It Out is an independent publishing platform focused on small press creative music releases and zine production. It was established because we've gotta let it out. Bigger productions take time and money. We've been there and we've found out that sometimes it's better just to press it small and be done with it. Good stuff deserves getting out there, we are all for it.

Gotta Let It Out was established in 2017 in Copenhagen by the musician Tomo Jacobson and the photo/video artist Malwa Grabowska aka. Hipermania.

We like mail. You can contact us at gottaletitoutnow@gmail.com.
